Bio

I am currently a D.Phil. student in Engineering Science at the University of Oxford, affiliated with Hertford College, under the supervision of Prof. Stephen Morris and Prof. Steve Elston. Previously, I received my M.S. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Southern California, advised by Prof. Zaijun Chen, and my B.S. in Physics from Nankai University, where I graduated Summa Cum Laude (Top 1%).

My research lies at the intersection of photonics and machine learning, with a focus on optical neural networks, ultralow-energy computing, and next-generation AI hardware systems. I am particularly interested in bridging physical computing architectures with efficient algorithm design, enabling scalable, high-performance, and energy-efficient intelligent systems.
